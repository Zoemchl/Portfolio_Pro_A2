In this group project, our goal was to think about the design streaming platform. We chose the theme of horror because a lot of ideas quickly came to mind to come up with an interface that would best suit our goals.

We have added many features to our platform to differentiate it from others, such as a screamer mode when searching for a program on the application. The idea was to make the user's journey memorable, original and fun to encourage them to use our application.
